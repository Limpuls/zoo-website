The animals

Would you like to know more about the animals in GIVSKUD ZOO?

At GIVSKUD ZOO, you can experience many different species. Almost one third of these species are endangered in the wild, and they therefore form part of an important international breeding programme involving zoos all over the world.

The lion (Panthera leo) is one of the big cats in the genus Panthera and a member of the family Felidae. The commonly used term African lion collectively denotes the several subspecies in Africa

With some males exceeding 250 kg (550 lb) in weight, it is the second-largest living cat after the tiger.
