Good to know

This section contains a wealth of information that is good to know before, during and after your visit. Use the search field on the front page to run free-text searches.

Dogs

Dogs are welcome at GIVSKUD ZOO, provided that they are kept on a lead at all times. However, dogs are NOT allowed on the Safari Bus, in the lion enclosure or in those animal enclosures where visitors come into direct contact with the animals (e.g. the Barbary macaques and the lemurs). There is a dog-sitting service adjacent to the entrance to the lion enclosure.

Emergencies/Acute illness

In the event of acute illness or emergency, contact the zoo staff IMMEDIATELY. All our staff are in radio contact with a qualified paramedic. The zoo has emergency medical equipment, including a defibrillator. To raise the alarm, call (+45) 75 73 02 22 or (+45) 21 65 28 47. If you call the emergency services directly, make sure that you also contact the zoo staff. We will guide the emergency services vehicle through the park so that you get help as quickly as possible.
